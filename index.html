My Research

My research is on Drone Detection using video processing. The main goal of the research is to build a robust and accurate drone detection system that can identify small UAVs, around sensitive areas like airports. It should also provide additional information about the behaviour of the drones and the possible danger they impose.

The main challenge of the project is to have a system that operates using low amount of power while maintaining the detection performance. Using current low power processors like GPU, FPGAs or other energy efficient massively parallel systems, such as the Graphcore IPU, we can optimise the detection algorithms for these architectures.
