About me

My name is Konstantinos Alexandridis and I am a computer vision research scientist at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab, in London, working under Jiankang Deng on multi modal large language models. I graduated from King's College London in 2024, under the supervision of Dr Shan Luo and Dr Anh Nguyen and my PhD thesis was about long-tailed image classification, object detection and instance segmentation. I did my masters in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Democritus University of Thrace, Greece.

I am a reviewer for many conferences such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ACCV and journals like International Journal of Coumputer Vision, IEEE-Transactions in Image Processing and Information Fusion. I was selected as an outstanding reviewer in ECCV2024 and CVPR2025.
