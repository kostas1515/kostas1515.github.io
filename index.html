My Research

My research focuses on large scale image recognition. In large scale image recognition, data are not balanced or curated because they are gathered in large volumes and annotation of all object instances is intractable. In this scenario, the data may be imbalanced, which means that there may objects that appear frequently in the dataset while there may be other that are unique and do not appear frequently. Under this realistic scenario, models trained with imbalanced data may have good performance in identifying the frequent objects of the dataset but they may fail to identify unique objects. My reseach aims to develop methods that tackle the long tailed recognition problem, so that the models can have better performance in detecting and recognising all the objects in the dataset.
